Prototype Google Maps Wheelchair Accessible Transit Route Checker

Best viewed on desktop/tablet. When given valid from/to points the app suggests whether Google route planner supplies an accessible or inaccessible route. Started at the #Hack4Access hackathon by @jimRsmiley and continued by Girl Develop It Philly's Summer of Open Source fellows Sally Kong, LeeAnn Kinney and Erin O'Brien.

Visit Unlock Philly for more information.
